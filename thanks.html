Thanks — you're on the list!

I got your sign-up. I review every one personally and send the TestFlight invites out myself, so this isn't an instant download — give it a little time (especially around trips and holidays; it's just me here).

What happens next

  1. I'll email you an Apple TestFlight invite — to the address you just signed up with. Keep an eye out for it (a quick peek in spam doesn't hurt).
  2. Install Apple's free TestFlight app first, from the App Store — that's how Apple hands out beta apps.
  3. Tap the invite and it opens Pilot Medical Guardian inside TestFlight. No invitation code needed — if TestFlight asks you for a code, you've opened it on its own; just tap the invite link from your email instead.

While you wait

Have a look at the FAQ — it covers what the app does, how your data stays private, how it's different from the services you may already use, and how to get started once you're in. Anything else, just email support@pilotmedicalguardian.com and it reaches me directly.
