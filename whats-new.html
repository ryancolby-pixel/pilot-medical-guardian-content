What's new, and what's next

Pilot Medical Guardian is built in the open with our beta pilots. Here's what's recently landed in the app and what we're working on now. The app is in private beta, so things move quickly — this page is a snapshot, not a schedule.

Recently shipped

Now in the beta

  • MedXPress autofill — fills your medications and doctor visits straight into the FAA form so you don't retype them every cycle.
  • CACI worksheet surfacing — when you log a diagnosis the FAA's CACI worksheets cover, the app pulls up the FAA's own documents-required checklist so you walk in prepared.
  • Prior-cycle answer check — snapshots each cycle's prep and flags when this cycle's answers drift from last time.
  • Special Issuance tracking — keeps your SI requirements, checklist, and physician letters organized, with reminders for recurring tests.
  • Health metrics vs. the FAA's own thresholds — charts your Apple Health data against the FAA's cited numbers, with the source shown.
  • Renewal countdown — calculates your certificate's expiration from the real 14 CFR rules and reminds you before it lapses.
  • AME directory — searchable, factual list of Aviation Medical Examiners, sortable by distance.
  • iPad layout — a proper sidebar on iPad, plus polish across the app.
What we're working on

In progress

Directions we're actively building. No promises on dates — beta priorities shift with tester feedback.

  • Apple Health Records import — pull your real medications and lab results from your healthcare provider, on your device, to speed up your prep.
  • Home Screen & Lock Screen widgets — a glanceable countdown to your next deadline: medical expiration, an SI test that's due, or a document that's expiring.
  • Siri & Spotlight — ask "when does my medical expire?" or jump straight to MedXPress prep.
  • Maps in the AME directory — see nearby AMEs on a map and get directions.
  • Faster visit entry — reuse a saved provider or duplicate a past visit in one tap.
  • Calendar-based reminders — add your renewal deadlines to your own calendar so it can remind you however you've set it up.

Found a bug or have a request? Beta testers can send feedback right from the app (About → Send feedback), or email support@pilotmedicalguardian.com.
